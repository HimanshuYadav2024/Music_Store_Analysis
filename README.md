# 🎵 Music_Store_Analysis_Using_SQL

## 📌 Project Overview
Analyzed a digital music store database using SQL to help the business 
understand its growth and answer key business questions.

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze the music store's data and extract actionable insights 
related to sales, customers, artists, and genres using SQL queries.

## 🗃️ Database Schema
The database contains 11 tables:
- Employee,
- Customer,
- Invoice,
- Invoice_Line
- Track,
- MediaType,
- Genre,
- Album,
- Artist
- Playlist,
- Playlist_Track

## 🔍 Questions Solved
**Easy Level**
- Who is the senior most employee?
- Which countries have the most invoices?
- Top 3 values of total invoice

**Moderate Level**
- Which city has the best customers?
- Who is the best customer?

**Advanced Level**
- Who listens to Rock music?
- Top 10 Rock artists by track count
- Tracks longer than average song length
